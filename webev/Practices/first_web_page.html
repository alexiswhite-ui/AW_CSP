Getting through your good and bad days!

When you play a high intesity sport there is so many things involved that often get looked over sadly enough. It is extremly important to be able to understnad that the small things will really mean the most in the long run, you'll thank yourself later I promise you.

In order to be a succesful volleyball player you need to be good to your body and mind both on and off the court. One of the most common things I see within all the athletes I have played with is the lack of training and preporation that these player upkeep. This could be doing stretches and basic workouts everyday to maintain the physical health you need to be out on the court, even taking mental days to work through things and seeking help when needed to keep you in the best shape. I have also seen this to be true in the off season. Many players I know play year round for fun or on teams, which is truly amazing, but the ones that don't do that might need to put in a little more of their own accoutabilty to prioritize working through things. Even as a coach it is important that you continualy keep up your knowlge of the game and physical performance to work with your players to get them through the season with progression enjoyably.
